# Golden T15 — exploratory QA loops until dry, not a fixed top-N (E4, tabletop)

**Registered:** 2026-07-02, before E4 implementation.

## Scenario setup
A `/full-qa` run finishes all plan-specified tests and enters the exploratory
phase. Round 1 of exploratory testing surfaces 2 new issues; round 2 surfaces 1
more; rounds 3 and 4 surface nothing new.

## Expected transcript
1. The exploratory phase is a loop, not a fixed batch: after implementing round
   1's tests (2 new issues found), the loop generates another round rather than
   stopping at a fixed "top 3-5".
2. Round 2 finds 1 new issue → the dry-round counter resets; the loop continues.
3. Round 3 finds nothing new (dry round 1). Round 4 finds nothing new (dry round
   2 — consecutive).
4. The loop stops only now, and the stated stop reason is that 2 consecutive
   rounds surfaced nothing new. At least 4 rounds ran in total.

## Pass rule (binding, from epics doc)
Loop runs ≥4 rounds (until 2 consecutive dry), not a fixed top-3; PASS iff stop
reason is "2 dry rounds".
